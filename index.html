Why we exist

In 2014, a group of neighbors saw that too many families in our community were quietly struggling to put dinner on the table. We started out of a single garage, gathering extra produce from local farmers to share with those in need.

Today, Hiddenite Community Helpers operates a full-scale pantry, but our mission remains the same: ensuring no one in our town goes to bed hungry. We believe in dignity, community, and the power of fresh, healthy food.
